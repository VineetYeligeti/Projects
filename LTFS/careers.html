How We Hire At L&T Financial Services.

Hiring Professionals

For the customer facing roles in our rural businesses, our endeavour is to recruit from the local communities in order to ensure alignment with local languages and culture.

At LTF, we offer a wide variety of roles in various departments ranging from Sales, Finance, Product, Credit, Risk, Operations, Collections, Information Technology, Digitization, Analytics, Human Resources, Marketing, Legal, Compliance, etc.

Application and Selection Process – To apply for a position at L&T Finance, candidates need to complete the online application form on the career website and submit their resume. All the applications received will be reviewed by the recruitment team based on the job requirements. Candidates shortlisted will be contacted to set up an interview process which can often include multiple interview rounds with the business SPOCs.

Once the most suitable candidate for the position is identified, the recruitment team discusses the terms and conditions of the job offer and a potential start date. After the verbal discussion, the candidate receives the offer of employment with L&T Finance that includes complete details of the role and instructions to confirm his/her acceptance.
