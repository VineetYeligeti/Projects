PROCESS FOR EMPANELMENT OF IFAS / PROPRIETORSHIP / PARTNERSHIP FIRMS / CORPORATE

For corporates (Both RIA and Otherwise):

Other than the documents mentioned below for empanelment, AMC and the distributor has to sign an agreement (combination of online and offline), that is available in the download section (Link). Post agreement, the file exchange with dummy transactions in the agreed format will be exchanged between the distributor and Karvy. Once that is approved by AMC, the distributor is allowed to transact. Once this agreement is signed the distributor is allowed to transact offline also.

The empanel form has to be completed in all respect and along with that the following documents needs to be submitted:
