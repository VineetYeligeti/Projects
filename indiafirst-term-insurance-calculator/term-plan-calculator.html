What IS  TERM PLAN CALCULATOR

A term insurance calculator is a tool that helps you determine the premium amount and coverage amount for a term insurance plan. It considers factors such as age, gender, smoking habits, and the desired policy term. Using a term insurance calculator can help you make an informed decision when buying a term insurance policy. Term insurance is a type of life insurance that provides coverage for a specific period of time, or "term." Term Insurance Plan helps you to provide financial security to your family, i.e., a predetermined amount known as 'death benefit', in case of their untimely demise during the policy term.

Frequently Asked Questions

How to calculate term insurance?

To calculate your term insurance requirement, determine the coverage you need keeping your current and future earning capacity, inflation, number of dependants, and long term financial commitments in mind. You can also use the online term plan calculator to determine your premium amount.

How to calculate term plan premium?

The online term insurance calculator can calculate your term plan premium, once you enter your age, gender, occupation, annual income, smoking habits, health conditions, the required sum assured, and the policy tenure.

Is term plan a good option?

Yes, term plan is a good option as it financially secures your family in the unfortunate event of your death, especially if you have debts or long term goals.

How many years term plan should I take?

While term plan tenures range from 5 to 40 years, you should opt for the maximum term that covers your financial requirements.

What is the best age to cover term insurance?

Term insurance is best bought when you are young as it gives you the benefit of low premiums and a higher coverage amount.

How is term insurance sum assured calculated?

Term insurance cover should ideally be equivalent to 10-15 times your annual income to cover inflation and provide for long term financial security.

What is the limit of term insurance?

Term insurance has no specific limit for the coverage amount. However, you will only be eligible for an amount basis your income and other factors.

What are 3 benefits of term insurance?

The top 3 benefits of term insurance are:
  • Financial security for your family
  • Additional riders that further cover you for critical illnesses and accidental death or disability
  • Tax savings

Does term insurance depend on salary?

Term insurance depends on income, whether you are salaried or self-employed.

How do I choose term insurance duration?

Before you choose your tenure, assess your current financial situation and liabilities, the financial status of dependents, and premium affordability. A long-term plan may be an unnecessary expense. Similarly, a short-term plan may have financial implications in case of your untimely demise.

How does your age affect your premium calculation?

Your health is an indicator of the premium you pay. The younger you are, the less chances of any complications and therefore, less risk. Hence, you can get a higher coverage at a low premium. If you buy term insurance when you’re older, you may have to pay a higher premium amount or opt for a lower coverage, depending on your health.

Why does occupation impact term insurance premium calculation?

People who work in high risk occupations, as defined by the insurer, can be charged a higher premium due to the risks associated with those jobs. If you want to know how to calculate term plan premium, use the online term insurance premium calculator.

What factors determine your term plan premium?

Your age, gender, occupation, annual income, smoking habits, and health conditions determine your term plan premium. You can use online term plan calculator to check this.

What is the impact of premium calculation on smokers?

Smokers may be charged a higher premium due to the health risks associated with the habit. If you smoke and want to know how to calculate term plan premium, use the online term insurance premium calculator.

What kind of death benefits are covered in this plan?

Term plans cover all health-related and natural deaths. The sum assured is paid as a lumpsum amount to the nominees in case of the policyholder’s demise during the policy term.

How to calculate sum assured for term plan?

Term insurance sum assured should ideally be equivalent to 10-15 times your annual income to cover inflation and provide for long term financial security. An online term insurance plan calculator can help you estimate the amount.
