Benefits of SIP

Affordable

  • You decide how much to invest.

Convenient

  • You can simply provide a one-time instruction to your bank to invest a pre-determined amount of money regularly towards your SIP contribution.
  • Multiple payment options:Auto Debit, NACH, PDC.

Power of compounding

  • Early and regular investing over a long time horizon helps to create wealth through compounding of returns.
  • You can start with a small sum as the key is not the size of the initial investment but the duration of investment.

Rupee cost averaging

  • It essentially helps you buy less when the markets are expensive and buy more when the markets are cheap.
  • Rupee cost averaging works out best in choppy markets.
