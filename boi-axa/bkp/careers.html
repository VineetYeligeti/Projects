Business development trainee/ Business development associate

Job Description

Business development trainee/ Business development associate (This is a contractual employment with 'Randstad India Ltd.')

Reporting to: Business Manager

Role & Responsibilities
  • Represent the BOI and AXA Investment managers for the mutual fund product. Maintain and build relationship with the customers of the branch.
  • Understand the, products, market scenario and suggest investment options based on the client profiling.
  • Manage 10+ branches of Bank of India
  • Manage walk-in clients as well as follow up with leads
  • Assist the Marketing Officer of BOI to complete the KYC for BOI AXA MF customers.
    Provide insights about the clientele of the branch to determine business potential.
Qualification & Skillsets

Minimum qualification - Graduate (any stream)

  • AMFI / NISM Certified preferred
  • Good Spoken & written English.
  • Good Mannerism & Etiquettes, Presentable
  • Understanding of the local language (E.g.: Maharashtra - Marathi, Karnataka - Kannada; etc.)
  • Better if has a private transport
  • Portrays Selling Skills - Skills to convert prospects to clients by closing initial sale and getting ongoing sales.

Industry : Insurance/ Banking/ Mutual Funds (1-3 years experience)

Job Locations : Nagpur

Traits Required:
  • Go Getter Attitude – Hunger to Perform and Grow.
  • Relationship Guy - Should be able to build and maintain relationships naturally – extroverted, has a habit of meeting new people and build relationships.
  • Ethical - No Rebating (or passing back commissions to investors) approach, Transparent and Open Client Relationships.
  • Integrity and Honesty – High Levels of Both.

Who can apply : Fresh graduates / MBA's, Candidates with 1-2 years of experience from - Insurance/ Banking/ Mutual Funds.

Only candidates matching the job requirements should apply.

Salary & Benefits offered:
  • Compensation at par with the market standards and experience
  • Mobile Reimbursement, Local Conveyance, Internet allowance & Medical Insurance

Gender: No preference

Age: 21 - 30 Years
